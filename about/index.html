The Studio

Designed at Pratt. Sharpened at W+K. Built in Brooklyn.

Reese Okonkwo studied design at Pratt, spent five years at Wieden+Kennedy, two more at Pentagram, and founded Aperture & Ink in 2022 to run smaller, sharper engagements with senior people in the room. Twelve people. One studio in DUMBO. No offshoring.

The Path

From Critique Wall to Client-of-Record

Pratt

BFA Communications Design

Pratt ’14. Senior thesis on regional packaging vernacular. Still references the same Tschichold lecture in studio crits.

2014–2019

Senior Art Director

Five years at Wieden+Kennedy NY. Led campaigns for an outdoor footwear brand, a beverage major, and one financial services account she still won’t name.

2019–2021

Associate Partner

Two years at Pentagram. Brand identity systems for a children’s book imprint and a regional grocery chain. Learned how to write a guidelines doc.

2022

Founded Aperture & Ink

Quietly stood up a 12-person studio in DUMBO, with a portal-first operating model and a deliberately small client roster.
