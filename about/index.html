Origin

From critique wall to client-of-record.

Pratt ’14. Senior thesis on regional packaging vernacular. Still references the same Tschichold lecture in studio crits.

2014–2019 · W+K NY. Five years as Senior Art Director. Led campaigns for an outdoor footwear brand, a beverage major, and one financial services account she still won’t name.

2019–2021 · Pentagram. Two years as Associate Partner. Brand identity systems for a children’s book imprint and a regional grocery chain. Learned how to write a guidelines doc.

2022 · Aperture & Ink. Quietly stood up a 12-person studio in DUMBO, with a portal-first operating model and a deliberately small client roster.

Aperture & Ink is a member of AIGA, the Type Directors Club, and The One Club for Creativity. Brand New “Notable Identity” 2024. Clockless “Built With Care” partner 2025.
